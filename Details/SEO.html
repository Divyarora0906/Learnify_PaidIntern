SEO Course

Description

Master the art of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) with our comprehensive course. Learn techniques to improve your website's visibility on search engines and drive organic traffic effectively.

Objective

To equip learners with the skills needed to optimize websites for search engines, increase organic traffic, and improve search engine rankings.

Prerequisites

  • Basic understanding of website functionality
  • Familiarity with digital marketing concepts

What will you Learn

  • Keyword Research: Identify and target the right keywords
  • On-Page SEO: Optimize website content and structure
  • Off-Page SEO: Build backlinks and improve domain authority
  • Technical SEO: Enhance website performance and indexing
  • SEO Analytics: Measure and analyze SEO performance
