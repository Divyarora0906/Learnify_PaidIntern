Artificial Intelligence (AI) Course

Description

Explore the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and learn how to build intelligent systems that can perform tasks requiring human-like cognition. This course provides a deep dive into essential AI components and real-world applications.

Objective

To equip learners with the skills to design, develop, and implement AI solutions using modern techniques and tools.

Prerequisites

  • Basic programming knowledge
  • Understanding of fundamental mathematical concepts

What will you Learn

  • Machine Learning: Build and train models to make predictions
  • Neural Networks: Understand and implement neural network architectures
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Process and analyze human language data
  • Computer Vision: Develop systems that interpret visual information
  • AI Ethics: Explore ethical considerations in AI development

Time Duration

  • 10 weeks
