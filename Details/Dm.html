Digital Marketing Course

Description

Dive into the world of Digital Marketing and learn how to effectively promote products and services online. This course covers various digital marketing strategies and tools to help you create and manage successful campaigns across multiple channels.

Objective

To teach learners the key techniques and tools used in digital marketing to enhance online presence and drive engagement.

Prerequisites

  • Basic knowledge of internet and social media platforms

What will you Learn

  • SEO: Optimize websites to rank higher on search engines
  • PPC Advertising: Manage pay-per-click campaigns effectively
  • Social Media Marketing: Create and execute strategies for social media platforms
  • Email Marketing: Develop and manage email marketing campaigns
  • Content Creation: Craft compelling content that attracts and retains customers

Time Duration
